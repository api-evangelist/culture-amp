The article explores Culture Amp's Performance Culture Quadrant (PCQ), a framework based on 1.5 billion employee survey responses showing that sustained high performance is a collective cultural property rather than an individual trait. Organizations combining high engagement with strong performance confidence achieve measurable outcomes, including 21% more high performers and 88% retention rates.
