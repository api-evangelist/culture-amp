The article argues that passive listening—using AI to collect and analyze existing workplace data—generates more buzz than actual organizational adoption. Culture Amp's research found that 80% of companies aren't using passive listening, with 63% saying it's not a priority. The authors warn that passive data contains hidden biases and recommend combining passive signals with active listening strategies rather than relying on them alone.
